Twilst for iPhone

Twitter Lists and Search with Tweet tagging.
Download on the App Store
Twitter Follow @twilst for updates, feedback and support!

Lists First.

Lists and Saved Searches are shown all within a single tap from the Home screen, with editing and managing just another tap away.

The Home Timeline, Direct Messaging and Mention Notifications have been left out altogether to focus on what Twilst is designed for - gathering information efficiently.

Tag Tweets.

Don't let another important Tweet go forgotten.

Tag any Tweet by long-pressing it on the Timeline, or from the dedicated Tag button on the Tweet's details screen.

Tagged Tweets are saved locally to your iPhone, so access is instant and always available.
